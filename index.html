Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

The principles that guide every decision, service, and initiative of Barangay Day-as.

🎯 Our Mission

Serving Every Resident with Excellence

To deliver responsive, transparent, and efficient barangay services that uphold the dignity and welfare of every resident. We are committed to fostering active community participation, ensuring equitable access to resources, and maintaining the highest standards of governance.

🌟 Our Vision

A Thriving, Inclusive & Progressive Barangay

To be a model barangay in Cebu — where every resident enjoys a safe, healthy, and prosperous life. We envision a community united by shared values, empowered by technology and innovation, and guided by honest leadership that prioritizes the common good.
