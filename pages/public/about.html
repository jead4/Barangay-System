🌱 Our Roots & Identity

Who We Are

Barangay Day-os is a thriving community dedicated to providing excellent public services and fostering a safe, sustainable environment for all its residents.

🌿

Origin

The name of our barangay traces its roots back to early settlers who named the area after their patron saint. Day-os was originally a vast agricultural land, well-known for its fertile soil and abundant natural springs that sustained the pioneering families.

Originally a vast agricultural land, it was known for its fertile soil and abundant natural springs that sustained the pioneering families who built the community from the ground up.

History

The name of our barangay traces its roots back to early settlers who named the area after their patron saint. Day-os was originally a vast agricultural land known for its fertile soil and abundant natural springs.

Over generations, the community grew and evolved into the thriving barangay it is today — with robust infrastructure, dedicated public servants, and an engaged citizenry committed to collective progress.
